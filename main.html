SFU Game Developer's Club

Welcome!

Welcome to the official website for SFU's game developer club! We love to support and teach all aspects of game development, be it art, programming, design. Swing by one of the weekly meetings in our Discord, or bring a project and get some snacks in person at the SFU Burnaby Campus.

Whether you have dozens of games under your belt or are just starting out, all levels of experience are welcome!
